What is The Reader's Space?

The Reader's Space is an online book club that has sprouted up across the country, breaking physical barriers and crossing border's to bring like minded bookworms and your favorite authors into your living room. Taking time to curl up in your favorite reading chair and devour a good book is one of life's small pleasure. But if you want to share your love for an author, discuss a book's thorny moral questions or just see some normal human faces, then the act of reading need not be isolated affair.

What do we do?

Our book club consist of hundreds and thousands of people who read and talk about book based on a topic or an agreed upon reading list. We focus on literary critique and on less academic topics and discuss about almost every genre from horror to romance and what not.

What to join The Reader's Space?

In a book club, you get to hold a coversation with people and no one is going to give you weird looks for acting like the characters are real people.Our book club is great way to establish a a sense of sense of community with other readers. You can spend hours chatting with people who love the same authors as you, or debating anout the same merits of particular character.
